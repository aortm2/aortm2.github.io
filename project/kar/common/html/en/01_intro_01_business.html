Director introduction

Labor Education Project

We contribute to the establishment of a mutual and respectful labor culture by promoting the understanding of proper labor relations and enhancing the ability to manage labor, as well as providing education for the vulnerable and labor rights gray areas for the prevention of labor disputes and the development of autonomous resolution capabilities.

Education fields
Civil service labor relations education
Fire and police labor relations education
Civil service labor relations education
Public institution labor relations education
Small business labor education
Special platform rights protection education
Professional instructor training (labor relations specialist, labor rights instructor)

School Education Project

We contribute to the establishment of advanced labor culture by promoting the awareness of labor rights and establishing rational labor relations.

Education fields
Education facility civil service labor relations education
Teacher labor relations education
Education civil service labor relations education
Youth and teenage employment labor education

Employment and Labor Ministry Task Education Project

We contribute to providing high-quality employment and labor administration services by providing education for the enhancement of task capabilities and professionalism for civil servants and personnel affiliated

Education fields
Employment and Labor Ministry Official and Affiliated Employee Capacity Development Training
Employment Promotion, Maintenance, and Creation Training for Employment Centers
Labor Supervisor Training
Industrial Safety Supervisor Training
Central Labor Commission Investigator Training

Employment and Labor Training Business

Contribute to improving the quality of employment and labor services nationwide by developing and providing systematic occupational counseling and employment support professional training and staff capacity building.

Training Fields
Private commissioned institution staff training
National Employment Support System personnel training
Employment counseling job training at the New Il Center

R&D Business

Contribute to improving the quality of employment and labor education by developing and providing context-specific education programs and contents and fostering awareness of employment and labor among the general public.

Development of Employment and Labor Training Programs
Publication of Employment and Labor Research Reports
Development of Employment and Labor Online Content
