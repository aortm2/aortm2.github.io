The 5 strategies 11 promotional work

  • Establishing a cooperative network for employment and labor education
    • ①Establishment of employment and labor education consultation system
    • ② Establishment of delivery system through networking of related organizations
  • Reinforcement of employment and labor education hub function
    • ③ Development of educational contents and strengthening of dissemination function

  • Digital-based education infrastructure innovation
    • ⑦ Establishment of digital hub platform for employment and labor education
    • ⑧ Online education assimilation
  • Future-oriented organization system innovation
    • ⑨Reorganized from operation to research and planning centered organization
    • ⑩Spreading Social Values for Employment and Labor Education
