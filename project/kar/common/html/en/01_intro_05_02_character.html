Character introduction

Main character (Doori)

  • Character concept

    A flower character who grows up eating knowledge about employment and labor.

    The flowers and grass above the head represent the step-by-step growth through education.

    As knowledge accumulates through education, the [Happiness Plant] above the character's head grows rapidly.

    Profile

    • Name : Doori
    • (The meaning of looking through employment and labor, and doing it together, not alone)
    • Height : 165cm
    • Age : Not able to estimate
    • Weight : Too light to measure
    • Personality: Cheerful, curious, kind
    • Preference: Exploring new places, socializing with people

Sub-character (Ggumi)

  • Character concept

    A character who constantly strives to reap the fruit of happiness through education.

    In the form of a plant seed, it shows how it grows rapidly in a pot called the Korea Employment and Labor Education Institute.

    Profile

    • Name : Ggumi
    • (It means helping your dreams come true)
    • Height : 70cm
    • Age : Impossible to know
    • Weight : 2kg (Can only measure the pot)
    • Personality: Passionate, positive, knowledgeable
