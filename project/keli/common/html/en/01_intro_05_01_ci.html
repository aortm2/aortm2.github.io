About KELI
CI introduction

KELI, the abbreviation of Korea Employment and Labor Educational Institute, was symbolized and combined with the logotype, and the square brackets ‘{’ are expressed in E so that they can be read naturally, and at the same time, through images that look like faces, it shows that it is a performing institution that communicates with the public and serves as a ‘link’ between education, employment, and labor.


The image of Korea Employment and Labor Education Institute, which is gradually growing through step-by-step colors, and each color contains various meanings and symbols.

Watermark

    • KELI Purple $ Green [KOREA] : Purple is a symbol of neutrality and harmony. Raising awareness of employment and labor among the people of the Republic of Korea and realizing a labor-respecting society

    • KELI Orange [Employment, Education] : Providing various opportunities as a bright future and educational space for Korea Employment and Labor Education Institute

    • KELI Red [Labor] : Contributing to equal opportunities in employment and labor education in Korea

    • KELI Navy [Institute] : Encouraging national job creation based on the expertise of educational institutions
