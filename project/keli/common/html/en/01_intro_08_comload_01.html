Directions to [Korea Employment and Labor Education Institute]

Directions by car

Gyeongbu Highway
  • Pangyo IC (toward Bundang) → Imae Intersection → Combined Heat and Power Plant → Neunggol three-way Intersection (turn left) → Munhyeong three-way intersection → Education Center three-way intersection (turn left, 3km) → Korea Employment and Labor Education Institute
Yeongdong Highway
  • Yongin IC (toward Gwangju) → National Road No. 45 → Hankuk Foreign Language Intersection (turn left) → Munhyeong three-way intersection (turn right) → Education Center three-way intersection (turn left after 3 km) → Korea Employment and Labor Education Institute
Jungbu Expressway
  • Gwangju Gyeongan IC (toward Gwangju) → National Road No. 45 → National Road No. 43 → Education Center three-way intersection (turn right after 3km) → Korea Employment and Labor Education Institute

Directions by public transportation

After getting off the public transportation (bus), it takes 50 minutes on foot to the Korea Employment and Labor Education Institute (approximately 2.5 km).

  • [Imaechon Hanshin. Seohyeon Station. AK Plaza] → Get on 1500-2 (toward Everland (ride home)) → Get off at [Korea Employment and Labor Education Center. Bonggol Entrance. Woorim Fusionville Apartment]
  • [Saemaeul Training Center entrance] → Get on 17-1 (toward Gwangju garage) → Get off at [Korea Employment and Labor Education Center. Bonggol Entrance. Woorim Fusionville Apartment]
