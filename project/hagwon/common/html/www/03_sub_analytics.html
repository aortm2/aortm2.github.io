FAST (Fitness of ACT vs SAT Tests)

Our FAST analytics is a ten minute 20-question quiz that helps students
choosing the better test between ACT and SAT.

  • Clear test choice guidance and its potentials

  • Readiness of either or both tests

  • A fitness report by test-prep experts

  • No need to spend hours after hours taking both exams.

  • Clear understanding of pros and cons of both exams.

  • STEP 1

    Read the instruction carefully and spend up to 10 minutes answering 20 questions.

  • STEP 2

    Once the questions are completed, a basic tendency graph will be generated.

  • STEP 3

    Enter your email for a full FAST report.
